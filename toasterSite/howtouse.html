How to Use a Toaster

  • Step
  • One
  • Two
  • Three
  • Four
  • Five
  • Six

1. Plug toaster into wall outlet

2. Insert what you want to put in the toaster into the slot that is closer to you of the two holes at the top.

3. Select the darkness of food in toaster using the adjustment knob.

4. Lower the lever to start the toasting cycle.

5. Wait 2-4 minutes

6. Take food out of toaster and put jam, honey, or anything else you may want to add onto it.
