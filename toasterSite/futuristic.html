Futuristic Toasters

  • Notepad Toaster
  • Portable Toaster
  • ROLLER Toaster

Notepad Toaster- Nothing says you love somebody quite like writing it out on toasted bread. The Notepad Toaster lets you write customizable message on the top and then those messages are toasted into the bread.

Portable Toaster- This handheld toaster works like a heated knife. Simply pass the portable toaster over the top of your toast and watch it brown before your eyes. A graphic interface on the back of the toaster changes as the toasts browns, letting you know how well the bread is toasted.

ROLLER Toaster- The ROLLERtoaster is the winner of the 2006 Red Dot Award, given out for achievements in design. Jaren Goh of Singapore created this compact toasting wonder. You insert the bread into one side of the toaster, and it rolls through the toaster to come out toasted on the other side.
