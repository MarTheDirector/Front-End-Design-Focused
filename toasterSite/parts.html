Heating Element *

Nicrhome wires, have about 900 watts of electricity running through them. Gets your toast nice and brown.

Lever *

Press down on this with your hand and the toast will lower between the 2 heating elements.

Circuit Timer *

A simple circuit that acts as a timer. When the peak voltage is reached through the resistor, the electromagnetic power is cut and the toast pops up.

Spring *

Bounces the toast back up after the latch releases when the toast has finished.
